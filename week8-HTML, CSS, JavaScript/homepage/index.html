ABOUT ME

I was born on August 6, 2002, in Ljubljana, Slovenia. I come from the village of Rudno, in the municipality of Železniki, where I have been helping on a farm and in a carpentry shop since I was a child. I completed a lower music school for the flute in Škofja Loka (9/2011-6/2017). After primary school, I participated in various projects at the art gymnasium, where I wrote, filmed, and directed various short films. After passing the general maturity exam at SVŠGUGL gymnasium, I continued my education at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science at the University of Ljubljana. Due to the desire to transfer from a university to a college program, I took a break in the 2022/23 school year, where I focused on work and gaining programming experience. While working, I expanded my knowledge with online Harvard education - CS50's Introduction to Computer Science.
